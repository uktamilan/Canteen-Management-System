Step 1: Connect to Wi-Fi

Enable Wi-Fi and connect to the Canteen ESP32 Network.

Step 2: Login via Register Number

Once connected, the login page will open. Enter your register number and D.O.B.

Step 3: Queue Position Assigned

Your queue number is assigned automatically. Wait for your turn.

Step 4: Place Your Order

When it's your turn, select food items and complete the payment.

Step 5: Receive QR Code

After a successful payment, you will get a QR Code.

Step 6: Go to QR Code Reader

Show the QR Code in the QR CODE READER and it give the recipt of your order.

Step 7: Collect Your Food

Give the recipt to the canteen staff and collect your food.
